About

the conference

The IEEE Nigeria Students Congress (STECON) 2022 is the first of its kind of physical conferences to be held in Nigeria for quite some time. With the theme Advancing the Future of Engineering and Technology through Youth Engagement, the conference will be held at the Digital Bridge Institute (DBI), Lagos, Nigeria from 9 - 11TH June, 2022. STECON2022 provides an opportunity for young engineers, tech enthusiasts, and IEEE Nigeria student branches to network, interact, exchange ideas, and demonstrate their skills and competencies.

The congress will feature industrial technical visits, panel & workshop sessions, an inter-SB quiz, a robotics competition, a hackathon, a technical paper contest, an engineering exhibition and more... Follow our social media pages @stecon2022 on Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook to get regular updates.
